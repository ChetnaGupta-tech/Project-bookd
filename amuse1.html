About The Show

Tired of stuffy humidity, high temperatures, and pollution? Escape the city to the pristine snowy landscapes of Switzerland! Plan a visit to the Snow Kingdom...Mumbai`s largest snow theme park! Three times the size of its closest competitor in Mumbai, Snow Kingdom is one of Asia`s finest and Mumbai`s largest full-fledged indoor snow theme park. As part of the attractions in R-City Mall, Ghatkopar, it is not only centrally located but also easily accessible. With exciting snow rides and the space to experience winter in a real way, Snow Kingdom is a must-visit attraction which promises a unique brand of fun. For the whole family!

Snowpark entry for 45 minutes

Sterilized winter gear (socks, shoes, jerkins, and gloves) on a returnable basis.

Of the 1-hour session, it takes 15 minutes to acclimatize to the lower temperature

Separate tickets must be purchased for DSLRs and digital cameras.
